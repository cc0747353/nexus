Our History

INTERNATIONALFASTEXPRESSfreight Company, our approach to courier services is unique. Our Courier Franchisees offer a cost effective, reliable, timetable courier service, backed up by the latest computer technology and online parcel track and trace facilities.INTERNATIONALFASTEXPRESS Company Couriers the ideal choice for individual consumers, small to medium sized businesses as well as leading international e-tailers.

Established in 1983 in 1 queen Elizabeth Ave,Hillington Glasgow G52 4NQ, United Kingdom.INTERNATIONALFASTEXPRESS Company is now a globally franchised courier company with over 30 years’ experience and a presence in five countries, China, Australia, Ireland, Northern Ireland and South Africa.
